About Us

Who We Are

GB Electricals is a trusted and approved electrical contractor delivering a comprehensive range of high-standard electrical services. We pride ourselves on skilled workmanship, technical expertise, and completing every project efficiently.

Our team of experienced, fully qualified electricians has provided professional services to domestic and commercial clients for over 10 years. We've successfully supported landlords, property managers, and businesses across London and Essex.
